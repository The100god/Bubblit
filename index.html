Bubblit Rules

  • For playing Bubblit click on Play.
  • In this game at the top given an heading Hit that indicate the number you have to hit in panal given at the below the top board.
  • When a number is appear on "hit panal".
  • Find the number on screen and click on it.
  • If you click on right number it adds 10 to your score.
  • And if you click on wrong number it deduct 10 from your score.
  • the game has time limit of 1 minute
  • At the top left we have Home button if you don't want to continue the game you can click on it and go back to home.
